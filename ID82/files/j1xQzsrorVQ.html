Rizky Febian or usually called Iky, was born in Bandung, February 25, 1998. Rizky Febian started his career early following in his father's footsteps in the world of entertainment and music. In 2015, Rizky Febian released his first single titled 'Kesempurnaan Cinta' which marked the beginning of Iky's career as a male solo singer. Iky released his first album in 2018 entitled 'JEJAK' and currently has 80 million streams on a digital music platform. Having a successful career in the music world, Rizky Febian built his independent label, namely RFAS Music, in 2020. Currently, RFAS Music is a place to explore himself in the world of music and entertainment. Rizky Febian hopes that RFAS Music can become a 'home' for other musicians who want to explore their music.
